Snake Football

Snake Football is a unique take on the classic snake game – but this time, instead of eating fruit to grow your snake, you will be playing football against another player. Control your snake with arrow keys, WASD keys, or your mouse and kick the ball into the other snake’s goal. You’ll get better at playing the game with each round, but the enemies will become more challenging as well. Each game lasts two minutes, so make sure you score as many goals as possible before the time ends.

As you play, you’ll be able to unlock new snakes – there are 6 snakes in total, each with a distinct appearance. You’ll need money to unlock new snakes, which you can earn by simply playing the game and winning stages. There are four different maps to play on, each with 5 stages of increasing difficulty to beat – or you can play against a friend in local multiplayer. 

Slither Snakes

How long can you grow your snake before you run into another player? In Slither Snakes, you’ll be controlling an intergalactic snake flying across the stars and eating little pellets that’ll extend your size. Your goal is to become the biggest snake in the galaxy – but other players are here as well to make it difficult. Whenever a snake runs into another, it drops all the pellets it has collected. 

Use your skills to ensnare other snakes and steal their pellets. Be careful – as you grow bigger, more and more players will come after your pellets. You can use the left mouse button to boost, but it’ll cause your snakes to drop pellets, which the other players can eat. Do you have what it takes to get the highest score and outgrow all the other snakes?

Forest Slither Snake

Collect fruits and candies to grow your snake in Forest Slither Snake, but watch out for other players who are out to get you. Like in classic Snake, you lose after you run into another player, so make sure to evade them whenever possible – or, if you can do it, have them run into you and steal all their points. 

You can customize your snake between games, choosing one of 16 available skins that represent different animals – from a cute bunny to an intimidating elephant. Try your best across 10 different levels, or play against others in multiplayer mode. Get the most points and don’t let the other players catch you off guard, or you’ll have to start from zero again. You can boost to get out of tight spots, but each use will cause your snake to shorten, making you an easier target.

Crazy Snake IO

Crazy Snake IO is yet another game of skill where you’ll have to steer your snake and avoid the opponents. This one is a bit more fast-paced, and your snake will zoom around the map with more speed than you’re probably used to. You can use that to your advantage, trapping the other unsuspecting snakes and taking their points away, but be careful – the other players can do the same to you. You can choose out of 14 available skins to customize your snake and stand out from other players. 

Pick between endless mode or timed mode. In endless, you can have as much fun as you want, trying to become the biggest snake the world has ever seen. In timed mode, you’ll have 4 minutes to gather as many points as you can, fighting against other snakes for domination. The player that is the longest when the 4-minute mark hits, wins the game.

Social Media Snake

Social Media Snake gives the classic snake game a new and unique look, decorating the entire world with social media emojis and icons. Collect these icons and grow your emoji snake to become the longest player in the game. Try to beat the high score and collect as many points as possible. You can customize your snake, selecting from a range of different emojis. To play, simply move your mouse around and the snake will follow. You can use the left mouse button for a short boost, but it will cost you some of your points.
